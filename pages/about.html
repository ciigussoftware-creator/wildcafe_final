Our Story

Nestled in the lush mountains of Ella, Wild Cafe is more than just a restaurant – it's a celebration of Sri Lankan culture and cuisine. Our journey began with a simple vision: to create a space where travelers and locals alike could experience the authentic flavors of Sri Lanka in a warm, welcoming environment.

From our famous hoppers and kottu to our signature 'go wild' dishes, every item on our menu tells a story of tradition, innovation, and love for good food. We work closely with local farmers and suppliers to ensure that every ingredient is fresh, sustainable, and of the highest quality.

Our Locations

Wild Cafe Ella (Ella)

A cosy jungle-style café in the heart of Ella (main street). Fresh breakfast, Lunch and Dinner offering hoppers, rotti, and 'go wild' comfort food.

Kandy Krush Hostel

Wild Cafe (Kandy)

Garden, pool, cafe, free WiFi, Chill lounge, outdoor space, Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner, private rooms, and free private parking. Close to Kandy Railway Station, Kandy Lake, and Sri Dalada Maligawa.

Tree House Hostel Sigiriya

Wild Cafe (Sigiriya)

Tree-house lodge with terrace garden, cafe, and free WiFi. 24h front desk, family rooms, and breakfast, lunch and dinner options. Near Sigiriya Rock, Pidurangala Rock, and Sigiriya Museum.
